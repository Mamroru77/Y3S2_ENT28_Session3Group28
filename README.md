## 🎯 Problem Framing & Vision
In university dormitories, conflicting lifestyles (sleep schedules, cleanliness, noise tolerance) often lead to severe interpersonal friction. 

**DormHarmony** is a student-led roommate screening platform designed to solve this. Instead of random allocation, we empower students to find their "soulmate roommates" through a customized weighted-matching algorithm, incorporating MBTI, daily habits, and personal boundaries. Our goal is to reduce dorm conflicts and foster a healthier campus living environment.

## ✨ Core Features (Sprint 1 MVP)

Based on our intensive development over the first 3-4 weeks, our current Web MVP includes:

* 📊 **Smart Dashboard:** An overview of your profile strength, potential matches, and weighted compatibility scores.
* 🛠️ **Custom Questionnaire Builder:** Users can create custom screening questions, assign **Weights (e.g., Weight 1-3)**, and define acceptable answers to filter out incompatible matches.
* 🔍 **Discover Roommates:** Browse through available roommate profiles. View their lifestyle tags (Quiet, Tidy, etc.), MBTI, and a detailed compatibility breakdown before sending a request.
* 🪪 **Digital Roommate Card:** A shareable public profile that clearly states personal boundaries (e.g., "Never stop my alarm", "Peeking at what others are doing").
